TEG
Description TEG is the largest shopping center in Albania, with 150 stores and 1,500 parking spaces. It offers exclusive brands and entertainment areas.
PADAM
Description Padam is an elegant restaurant in Tirana offering a high-class culinary experience in a villa from the 1930s, with a variety of wines from around the world.
Eat Your Way Through Tirana's New Bazaar
Description The New Bazaar is a renovated market offering fresh fruits, organic vegetables, local wine, and raki. It connects to Skanderbeg Square.
Watch The Sunset On Top Of Dajti Mountain
Description Dajti Mountain offers a peaceful escape from the city, with a cable car ride that takes you to the top for stunning sunset views.
Go On A Day Trip To Kruje Castle
Description Kruje Castle, located in the town of Kruje, was once the stronghold of the national hero Skanderbeg. It offers a fascinating historical experience.
